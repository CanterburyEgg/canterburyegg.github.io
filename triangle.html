    How to Play

    • The Goal: Connect the 3 actors by naming the movies they starred in together.
    • The Board: You start with one actor revealed. Guess the movie that links them to the hidden actors to reveal the rest of the triangle.
    • Strikes & Clues: Every wrong guess is a Strike. Strikes unlock hints to help you:
      • 1 Strike: Second Actor
      • 2 Strikes: Release Year
      • 3 Strikes: Third Actor
      • 4 Strikes: Supporting Cast
      • 5 Strikes: Plot Summary
    • Game Over: If you get 6 Strikes before completing the triangle, you lose!
